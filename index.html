About Our Company

At Working Hands Electric, we put heart and honesty into every job. Our team is respectful, professional, and treats every home like it's our own. We're proud to be COVID-conscious, clean, and loved by customers for our transparency. We don't fake it just to make it. We're real. And reliable.
